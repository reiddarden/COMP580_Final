Story

While on a school field trip exploring the Morehead Planetarium, you happen to find UNC's mascot Rameses on the loose. It just so happens to be the day of the big UNC-Duke Basketball game across campus at the Dean E. Smith Center. Your goal is to navigate Rameses across campus, visiting the many notable landmarks, before returning him to the Dean Dome to help lead the Tar Heels to victory!
